Christ Episcopal Church Website Re-Design Discovery Report

By Jamie Wilson

  • Site Intent
  • Competitive Analysis
  • Targeted Audience

Site Intent

The intent of this project is a re-design of Christ Episcopal Church’s website. The re-design will include enhanced site structure using validated HTML and CSS standards, responsive design, and improved aesthetics. A new logo will be designed as the current logo is displayed in plain text. Navigation will be completely redone because the current configuration is not organized and does not convey a clean look.

There are no connections to social media of any kind on the current site. Additionally, the site does not currently display well on mobile devices. Both of these issues will be addressed in the re-design.

Competitive Analysis

As this site is affiliated with a religious organization, its only competition is other Episcopal Churches, all of which are non-profit. An inspection of the competitor’s sites showed a clean, polished and organized look with a smooth web 2.0 feel. All competitors had links to social media sites on their home pages and intuitive navigation controls. Additionally, graphics and photos were higher quality and higher resolution.

A local (Florida) Google search query found the site within the top 10 results (#9).

Targeted Audience

The targeted audience for Christ Episcopal Church (using info gathered from similar sites as no data was available for the specific site) is Caucasian males and females age 50 and older, in the United States. They have high levels of education and generally earn 100k or more per year. They are also, obviously, affiliated with religious organizations, specifically, Episcopalian. Users typically spend very short amounts of time on the pages of the site.
